Introduction

Hello!

I am a 4th year undergrad Computer Engineering major at the University of Florida (go gators!) and am looking for opportunities with a stimulating environment, which will challenge me to constantly learn and become a better Engineer. From developing a client-server based remote laboratory system, to building an embedded JSON parser, I am always looking for new and fun ways to tinker with what can be done in the realm of Software Development. I find great enjoyment from working on small one-off passion projects, such as my antialiasing tool or RPG game, and seeing where they take me and what I can learn. However, I am no stranger to agile development and longer project timetables with multiple sprints and enjoy those overarching projects just as much.

On a personal note

I love to visit new places, and to bake food with my friends. I think cooking with friends is a great way to spend time when at home, and really makes for a lot of fond memories. However, I do like to leave my local area every now and then. I've visited a lot of countries, and even studied abroad in Denmark for a semester! I would love to be able to travel more once I graduate and it's something I do enjoy. I'm excited to see where I'll end up moving after graduation...maybe with your company!
